★Café★

Cafés are types of restaurants which predominantly serve coffee and baked goods. Theyre popular social settings and are often times a place for casual work or relaxation as opposed to a usual restaurant setting which is more strictly for eating. Here you will see some popular kinds of food and drink which you can expect to find in a Café. More information can be found at: This site.

Croissants Coffee Donuts Juice

A croissant is a flaky pastry made from folding layers of dough and butter together, eventually made and baked into a crescent shape. Its a popular pastry which originates from France.

Coffee is a drink made by brewing ground coffee beans in water. This creates a dark and bitter drink which is either drank black as is, or combined with some kind of flavoring, sugar, and/or cream in order to enhance the flavor or take some of the bitterness away. Its a popular drink because of its caffeinating properties, making it a common drink in the morning, and is always served in a Café.

Donuts are a kind of pastry made from frying dough. While they can be eaten plain, donuts are often accompanied with a variety of toppings including but not limited to, sugar, frosting, fruit, etc. Donuts have early origins in Latin America, but were popularized greatly in England and North America. Theyre extremely popular and are paired often with coffee, making them a common Café pastry.

Juice is a drink made from pressing fruits and/or vegetables in order to extract their natural liquids. Theyre a refreshing way to consume fruits and are a popular morning time drink, which means they commonly are sold in Cafés as an alternative to the more popular coffee.
